Affordable Quality Housing for Every Family

Low-cost prefab houses are a durable and cost-effective alternative to traditional construction, with options ranging from simple tiny homes to expandable family residences.

The final price depends on the size, design, and materials, but prefabricated construction significantly reduces the labor and material waste associated with conventional builds — passing those savings directly on to you.

Each cottage is built using high-quality prefab components manufactured under strict quality controls, then assembled on-site with precision by our experienced construction team.

Benefits

Why Choose Prefab Construction?

Prefabricated construction offers significant advantages over traditional building methods.

💸

Cost Effective

Prefab construction reduces material waste and labor costs significantly, making quality housing accessible at lower prices.

⚡

Faster Build Time

Factory-manufactured components mean on-site assembly is much quicker than conventional construction — move in sooner.

🌱

Eco-Friendly

Reduced waste, optimized material usage, and energy-efficient designs make prefab housing the sustainable choice.

🔒

Quality Controlled

Components manufactured in controlled factory environments ensure consistent quality that exceeds on-site construction standards.

📐

Customizable

Every prefab package can be customized to your specific requirements, land size, and design preferences.

🏆

Durable

Built to last with high-quality materials and structural integrity that meets or exceeds traditional construction standards.

Quality Assured

Standard Material Specifications

Our buildings are constructed using premium quality materials for maximum durability and safety.

🏗️ Foundation & Structure

  • Foundation: RCC (Reinforced Cement Concrete) & grade beams
  • Framework: Light steel columns, beams & purlins (MS Plate Grade 50)

🧱 Walls & Roof

  • Outer Walls: 2.5" to 3" RC concrete or solid blocks
  • Inner Walls: 2" to 3" concrete solid blocks
  • Roofing: 0.46mm colour-coated steel sheet or 3" RC concrete

🚪 Doors & Windows

  • Main Doors: 7'x3' steel frame with wooden shutters
  • Inner Doors: 7'x2.5' PVC frame & shutters
  • Windows: Thai aluminum frames with glass & safety grills

✨ Finishes & Utilities

  • Floors: 16"x16" glass-finish tiles & 2.5" RC sub-floor
  • Paint: High-grade weather coat & plastic paint
  • Electrical: Concealed BRB cables, standard points
  • Sanitary: Premium metal fittings with high commodes
