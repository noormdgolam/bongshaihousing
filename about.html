Our Story

Who We Are

BONGSHAI HOUSING is a Bangladesh-based Real Estate and Housing Company. The company undertakes projects on the basis of Engineering-Procurement-Construction & Commissioning (EPC&C).

We have the experience and resources to execute complex projects on time with international standards of quality, safety, and reliability. The company always gears up to excel stakeholders' expectations with its modern technology and sophisticated, experienced professional workforce.

BONGSHAI HOUSING will be responsible for the entire process of the project, ranging from design and materials supply to construction/installation and test operation. We maintain a great deal of experience in preparation, co-ordination, implementation, new-build, and maintenance works.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver world-class, affordable, and innovative housing solutions across Bangladesh — providing every family and business with access to quality construction built on the pillars of integrity, safety, and excellence. We aim to be the most trusted EPC housing company, creating lasting value for all stakeholders through innovation and commitment.

🔭

Our Vision

To become South Asia's most respected prefab and real estate company — recognized globally for transforming the housing landscape with cutting-edge technology, sustainable construction practices, and an uncompromising dedication to client satisfaction. We envision a Bangladesh where quality housing is accessible to all.
